Find :-

db.students.find({name:"Bob"})
db.students.findOne({age:23})

-

Find with Projection :-

db.students.find({},{name:1,course:1,_id:0})

db.students.find({}).projection({name:1,course:1,_id:0})

-

Find with Count :-

db.students.countDocuments({age:23})

-

Find with Sort :-

db.students.find().sort({name:1})
db.students.find().sort({name:-1})

-

Find with Limited records :-

db.students.find().limit(3)

db.students.find().limit(3).skip(3)
